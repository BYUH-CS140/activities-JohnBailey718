Description: Upgrade your instant ramen with a few fresh additions. Ingredients (Serves 1): 1 pack instant ramen 2 cups water 1 egg (optional) 1/2 cup frozen veggies or chopped greens Soy sauce, sriracha, or sesame oil (optional) Instructions: Bring water to a boil. Add noodles and vegetables. Simmer for 2 minutes. Add seasoning packet. Crack in an egg, poach for 1-2 minutes without stirring. Stir, serve hot, and top with sauce if desired.
