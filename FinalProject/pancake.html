Description: Light, fluffy pancakes perfect for weekend mornings. Ingredients (Makes 8 pancakes): 1 cup all-purpose flour 2 tbsp sugar 2 tsp baking powder 1/4 tsp salt 1 cup milk 1 egg 2 tbsp melted butter or oil Instructions: In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter. Combine wet and dry ingredients—don’t overmix. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake. Flip when bubbles form. Cook until golden. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or butter.
