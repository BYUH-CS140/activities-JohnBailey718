Ingredients (Serves 2): 4 eggs 2 tbsp milk or cream Salt & pepper to taste 1 tbsp butter Instructions: Crack eggs into a bowl. Add milk, salt, and pepper. Whisk until well combined. Melt butter in a nonstick pan over medium heat. Pour in the egg mixture. Let it sit for a few seconds. Gently stir with a spatula until curds form and eggs are just set. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
