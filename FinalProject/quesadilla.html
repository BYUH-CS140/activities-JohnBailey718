Description: A cheesy, melty tortilla favorite—quick and customizable. Ingredients (Serves 1–2): 1 large flour tortilla 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, etc.) Optional: cooked chicken, beans, peppers Instructions: Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place tortilla in pan. Sprinkle cheese over half. Add optional fillings. Fold tortilla in half and press down. Cook 2–3 minutes per side until golden and cheese melts. Slice and serve with salsa or sour cream.
