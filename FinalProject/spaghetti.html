Description: A classic pasta dish with savory tomato sauce. Ingredients (Serves 3–4): 8 oz spaghetti noodles 2 cups marinara sauce (jarred or homemade) 1 tbsp olive oil 1 garlic clove, minced Grated parmesan (optional) Instructions: Boil water with salt. Cook spaghetti until al dente. Drain. Heat olive oil in a pan. Sauté garlic for 30 seconds. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes. Toss in pasta. Serve with parmesan if desired.
