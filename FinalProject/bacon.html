Description: Crispy, savory bacon cooked just the way you like it. Ingredients (Serves 2): 6 slices of bacon Instructions (Stovetop): Lay bacon in a cold skillet. Do not overlap. Turn heat to medium. Cook slowly, flipping occasionally. Remove when crispy. Drain on paper towels.
